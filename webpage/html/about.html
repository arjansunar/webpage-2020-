About

Visit Nepal 2020 : Nepal has announced 2020 as visit nepal year aiming to 2 million tourist per year. Nepal has always been in the bucket list of many of the tourist. Nepal has been blessed by it's natural beauty and it's diversified inhabitation making it rich both physically and culturally. Nepal is blessed with many heritages like : Tallest mountain in the world, phewa lake. Nepal is not only rich by it's physical beauty but it is equally decorated by it's culture. To establish a unique identity in the world and promote the export of nepalese handicraft, Visit Nepal 2020 could be the best oppourtunity. This website is intended to provide information to tourist so that they can have idea of what places they can visit and what they can do here. Along with that we have Nepal yatra section in our website as well so that they can take a virtual tour to few places as well.
